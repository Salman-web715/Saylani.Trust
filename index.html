Saylani Welfare International Trust is an NGO established in 1999,

It was founded by Maulana Bashir Farooq Qadri. The trust focuses on feeding the poor and homeless, providing education, healthcare, and emergency services. It operates over 100 centers, mainly in Karachi, providing food to over 30,000 people daily headquartered in Karachi, Pakistan.
